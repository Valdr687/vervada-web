The Vervada

The Vervada is a vanilla friendly medieval texture pack for Minecraft ! The main idea behind the creation of The Vervada is to highlight the medieval fealing of the game, by making textures more rustic. But also by making the game more realistic ! The Vervada add a lots of variants and 3D models for a more immersive experience.
